About Japan

Where is Japan?

Japan is located in East Asia.

This air travel distance from USA is equal to 6,321 miles. It takes 11.29 hours to arrive.

Japan has only half the land area of Texas.However, people live about five times the population of Texas (about 130 million).

Japan consists of 5 main groups of islands; Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu, and Okinawa.

Tokyo is the capital city of Japan.

What is the annual climate like in Japan?

The climate in Japan varies greatly depending on the region and time of year. In general, Japan experiences four distinct seasons, similar to the United States.

However, Japan is relatively prone to rain throughout the year, especially from June to July when rainfall occurs continuously As a result, humidity levels can reach nearly 80% during the summer months. Therefore, taking measures to manage humidity is important for Japanese housing.

I generally recommend traveling to Japan in spring. The climate is relatively stable, not too hot, and you will see the country adorned with cherry blossoms, tinting it pink everywhere you go.

What is the main transportation?

People living in urban areas use trains to get around.

In addition, the Shinkansen (bullet train) runs through Japan, and it is possible to travel most main places in the mainland by this means of transportation.

(Of course, cars are also one of main transportation.)

Greeting style

In Japan, bowing is a more common greeting than shaking hands, and it is a gesture that signifies respect towards others. Surprisingly, bowing is utilized in various situations, including showing appreciation, apologizing, and greeting.
